Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock - Game Rules

The game is an expanded version of the classic Rock Paper Scissors game. The rules are as follows:

  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Scissors decapitates Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Spock vaporizes Rock
  • Rock crushes Scissors

To play, simply click on one of the choices and see who wins the round!
